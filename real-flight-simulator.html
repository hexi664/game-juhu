Real Flight Simulator

Experience the thrill and challenge of piloting aircraft in this realistic flight simulator. Take control of various planes, navigate through different weather conditions, and complete exciting missions as you hone your flying skills.

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Throttle: Use W (increase) and S (decrease) keys to control the throttle.
  • Yoke (Steering): Use A (left) and D (right) keys to turn the aircraft.
  • Pitch: Use the up and down arrow keys to control pitch (nose up/down).
  • Landing Gear: Press G to toggle the landing gear.
  • Camera View: Press C to cycle through different camera views.

Flight Mechanics

  • Takeoff: Increase throttle to maximum, pull back gently on the yoke when reaching sufficient speed.
  • Cruising: Maintain level flight by balancing throttle and pitch controls.
  • Landing: Reduce throttle, extend landing gear, and approach runway at a gentle angle.
  • Flight Instruments: Monitor your altitude, airspeed, and heading indicators to navigate safely.

Tips for Success

  • Start with basic training missions to learn the controls before attempting complex flights.
  • Be gentle with the controls - sudden movements can cause aircraft instability.
  • Plan your approaches and landings well in advance.
  • Learn to use the various camera angles to improve your spatial awareness.
  • Pay attention to weather conditions as they significantly affect flight characteristics.
