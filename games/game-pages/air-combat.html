Air Combat

Air Combat is a fast-paced shoot 'em up game where you control an aircraft to dodge enemy fire, defeat opponents, and achieve the highest possible score in this thrilling aerial battle.

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Movement: Use the left and right arrow keys to move your aircraft horizontally.
  • Firing: Press the spacebar to shoot at enemy aircraft.
  • Goal: Survive as long as possible while destroying enemy aircraft and collecting power-ups.

Game Mechanics

  • Enemy Aircraft: Different types of enemy aircraft will appear with varying movement patterns and health points.
  • Power-ups: Collect yellow power-ups to increase your firing rate and green ones to restore your health.
  • Health: Your aircraft has limited health. When hit by enemy fire or collision, your health decreases.
  • Score: Each enemy destroyed increases your score. Larger or more difficult enemies yield higher scores.

Tips for Success

  • Keep moving to avoid enemy fire – staying in one place makes you an easy target.
  • Prioritize collecting power-ups, especially when facing waves of stronger enemies.
  • Focus on destroying enemies at the top of the screen first to reduce incoming fire.
  • Learn the patterns of different enemy types to anticipate their movements and attacks.
