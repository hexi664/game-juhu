Stickman GTA City

  1. Home
  2. Stickman GTA

Explore an open-world city as a stickman character in this action-packed game. Complete missions, drive vehicles, and cause mayhem while navigating the urban environment with complete freedom.

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Movement: Use WASD keys to move your character throughout the city.
  • Combat: Aim with your mouse and click to shoot at targets.
  • Vehicles: Approach a vehicle and use the interaction key to enter and drive it.
  • Goal: Complete missions, explore the city, and survive in this open-world environment.

Game Mechanics

  • Missions: Various missions are available throughout the city, providing objectives and rewards.
  • Weapons: Different weapons can be found or purchased, each with unique characteristics.
  • Vehicles: A variety of vehicles can be driven, including cars, motorcycles, and more.
  • Police: Criminal activities will attract police attention, increasing your wanted level.

Tips for Success

  • Explore the city thoroughly to discover hidden items and shortcuts.
  • Complete missions to earn money and unlock new content.
  • When your wanted level is high, find a hiding spot or change your appearance.
  • Master driving skills to navigate the city quickly and escape pursuits.
